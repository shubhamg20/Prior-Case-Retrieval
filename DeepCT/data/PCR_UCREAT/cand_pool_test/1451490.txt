The notice of hearing issued to the respondents received back with the postal remark Left <ORG> has filed this appeal against the Order in Appeal dated passed by the Collector Appeals A show cause notice was issued to the respondents on the ground that Ball Roller bearings are not parts of <ORG> pumps but these are inputs of these pumps The respondents filed an appeal and the Commissioner Appeals allowed the appeal on the ground that the adjudication order travelled beyond the scope of the show cause notices because the benefit was proposed to be denied on one ground but it was denied on other ground In view of the above discussions we find no merit in the appeal
