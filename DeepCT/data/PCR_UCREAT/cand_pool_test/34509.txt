This is an application in revision against the order of the Subdivisional Magistrate Khagaria dated the th December in a proceeding under Section They may be taken into consideration in support of one or the other version given by one or the other party but the documents by themselves cannot and do not establish possession To that extent his judgment is entirely vitiated and it must be said that there is no legal basis for the finding of possession recorded by the learned Magistrate In the result this application is allowed the order of the learned Magistrate dated the th December is set aside and the case is sent back for disposal in accordance with law
